Old Man Juancho

Released on July 17, 2022

Entropius, the die, was an entry for GMTK Jam 2022. The theme was Roll of the Die. For this game, none of my usual friends and contributors were able to help. Since I had experience doing pixel art from Waking Graves, I decided to do everything myself.

I feel quite happy with the result; the game obtained a #1730 spot in creativity out of 6,000 entries, and overall it was 3145. I did everything myself, and I focused more on the UX, which was something I missed in a lot of my previous jam entries. This actually subtracted from my level of design time, but I think people liked the concept enough.

I kept everything mostly monochromatic so I wouldn't spend too much time thinking about color theory or how to design the characters, and the die was actually a godsend in that regard.
