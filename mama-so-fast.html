Mama So Fast

released on April 21, 2018. This game was created for a contest on puzzle games; the deadline was pretty close, so we decided to take a game-jam-style approach to its development.

My Contributions:

  • Project Lead: I determined what was going to make the final cut and what wasn't, and I assigned tasks to my two co-developers: a designer and another developer
  • Core gameplay design: I implemented the grid-based movement system. I also created a script to read text files with matrix representations of levels so that the designer could rapidly mock levels and they could be tested in-game. I implemented the sound and scene management scripts

first "finished" project. I learned a lot from implementing the grid creation system, and after its development, I tended to develop games using pixel art and grid-based puzzle games, since I found the experience extremely satisfactory. It was also an enriching experience managing a team over a short span of time with big effort pushes.
