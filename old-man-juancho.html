Old Man Juancho And Hist Yet to be found creature 3d Adventure & Knuckles

Released on February 1, 2021.

Developed for Global Game Jam 2021. The theme was "Lost and Found". The Name is a Joke on the naming conventions of the Sega Genesis' Sonic games. For the lost and found we started with the idea of having two characters: Lost and Found which were trying to reconnect, it eventually devolved into a lovecraftian story (as most things do) and we had an old captain trying to escape an ancient prison which also holds a eldritch horror prisoner which require's Juancho's help to escape. The core idea was that you would use audio cues to find traps that the horror could deactivate but required the captain's input to use its powers.
