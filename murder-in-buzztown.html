Murder In Buzztown

To be released on December 10, 2023. Development started in September 2023.

This game was my third project as a lead in HomeTeamGameDev. By this time I've come to understand a lot of my shortcomings regarding development, so I focused on creating a short proof of concept of a genre I like a lot (metroidvanias) with a mixture of things I like a lot (detective series), and while the outcome is more of a platformer (due to the time restrictions), I think it lays a solid foundation if I want to keep developing the idea.

This has also been the project where I've been most consistent with my contributions (I'd estimate I've spent 6–8 hours weekly) and where I've been more conscious of my choices in art and level design. I still wish I could improve things (like the enemy AI), but overall, I am quite proud of what I have accomplished.
